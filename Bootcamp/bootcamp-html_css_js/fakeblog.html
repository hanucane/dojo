Soto Dojo Blog

Eric Soto is a rugby player based in Seattle, WA. He plays with a local British Columbia Premiership team, the Seattle Saracens. He is a graduate of SUNY Stony Brook University with a B.S. in Athletic Training. He is currently working to join the computer science community by gaining a more thorough understanding of coding languages.
